About MapDraw

MapDraw is a mapping application for creating poles and cantons on an OpenStreetMap-based map.

Features:
  • Add poles to the map (minimum 50cm apart)
  • Create cantons by connecting poles
  • Data persisted in browser localStorage
  • Works fully offline
How to Use:
  1. Click "Add Pole" to place poles on the map
  2. Click "Add Canton" then click poles to connect them
  3. Double-click or change tool to finish drawing
